About QR Hunt

The exciting QR code treasure hunt game

What is QR Hunt?

QR Hunt is an interactive game that combines the excitement of a treasure hunt with the convenience of QR code technology. Players scan QR codes to discover prizes, special offers, and more!

How to Play

Playing QR Hunt is simple and fun:

  1. Look for the floating QR codes on the screen
  2. Scan the QR code with your device
  3. Follow the link to see if you've won a prize
  4. The more you play, the better your chances of winning!

Exciting Features

  • Animated QR codes that move around the screen
  • Colorful visual effects to enhance your gaming experience
  • Instant prize notifications
  • A variety of prizes to be won
  • No downloads required - play directly in your browser

Prizes and Rewards

QR Hunt offers a variety of exciting prizes and rewards:

  • Cash prizes
  • Gift cards
  • Discount coupons
  • Special offers from our partners
  • And many more surprises!

Remember, the more you play, the better your chances of winning. Someone wins a prize approximately every 20 seconds on QR Hunt!
