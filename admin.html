AdSense Setup Instructions

  1. Sign up for Google AdSense if you haven't already.
  2. Once approved, replace the placeholder AdSense code in the ad-landing.html file with your actual AdSense publisher ID.
  3. Replace ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX with your publisher ID.
  4. Create ad units in your AdSense account and replace data-ad-slot="XXXXXXXXXX" with your actual ad slot IDs.
  5. Test the ads to ensure they're displaying correctly.
  6. Monitor your performance both here and in the AdSense dashboard.

Note: The revenue estimates shown here are based on an average RPM of $3.00, which is a conservative estimate. Your actual earnings may vary based on factors like traffic quality, geographic location, and ad placement.
