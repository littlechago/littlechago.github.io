Our Story

QR Hunt was created in 2023 with a simple mission: to make QR codes fun and rewarding. What started as a small project quickly grew into an engaging platform that combines the excitement of treasure hunting with the convenience of QR technology.

Our team of developers and designers worked tirelessly to create a visually stunning experience that keeps users coming back. The floating QR codes, falling confetti, and money animations are all part of our commitment to making QR Hunt not just a game, but an immersive experience.

Today, QR Hunt is enjoyed by thousands of users worldwide, with new features and prizes being added regularly. We're proud to have created a platform that brings joy to so many people while introducing them to exciting new content across the web.
