How to Play QR Hunt

1

Visit QR Hunt

Open QR Hunt on any device with a camera. No downloads or installations required - it works right in your web browser!

2

Follow the Floating QR Code

Watch as the QR code floats around your screen, surrounded by falling confetti and money animations. The code changes every few seconds, so be quick!

3

Scan or Click the QR Code

On mobile devices, use your camera to scan the QR code. On desktop, simply click the code to activate it.

4

Discover Your Result

Each scan has a chance to win real prizes worth up to $100! Even if you don't win immediately, you'll discover interesting content and can try again.

5

Claim Your Prizes

If you win, follow the instructions to claim your prize. Prizes range from gift cards to digital downloads and exclusive discounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is QR Hunt free to play?

Yes, QR Hunt is completely free to play. There are no hidden fees or subscriptions required.

How often can I win prizes?

Each scan gives you a chance to win, and someone wins approximately every 20 seconds! The more you play, the better your chances.

What kinds of prizes can I win?

Prizes include Amazon gift cards, digital downloads, discount codes, free trial subscriptions, and entries into larger sweepstakes.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to play, but creating one allows you to track your progress and claim certain prizes more easily.

How do I claim my prizes?

If you win, you'll be directed to a prize claim page with specific instructions for your particular prize.
